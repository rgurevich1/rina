About me

I'm a Graphic Designer specializing in Brand Identity, UX/UI Design, and Digital Multimedia Design. I earned my Bachelor of Design in Digital Multimedia Design from Pennsylvania State University, where I graduated with a 3.8 GPA.

Throughout my career, I have developed a strong passion for creating meaningful visual experiences that help businesses communicate their message effectively and connect with their audiences. My expertise includes brand identity development, logo design, UX research, wireframing, prototyping, content planning, illustration, and visual storytelling.

Currently, I work as a Brand Identity Designer at Holding Advisory, LLC, where I have been helping clients establish and strengthen their brands since 2022. In this role, I consult on brand strategy, create logos and visual identities, design podcast cover artwork, edit audio, and contribute to UX/UI design projects. What I enjoy most about this work is the opportunity to combine creativity with problem-solving — helping businesses communicate their values through thoughtful design.
